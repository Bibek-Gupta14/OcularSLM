Shopping List for a Month

GROCERIES:
- Milk: 4 liters (1 liter per week)
- Eggs: 2 dozen (6 per week)
- Bread: 4 loaves (1 per week)
- Rice: 5 kg
- Pasta: 3 packs (500g each)
- Chicken: 3 kg
- Beef: 2 kg
- Fish: 2 kg
- Vegetables: Assorted (buy weekly: onions, potatoes, tomatoes, carrots, leafy greens)
- Fruits: Assorted (buy weekly: apples, bananas, oranges, seasonal fruits)
- Yogurt: 8 cups (2 cups per week)
- Cheese: 1 kg
- Butter: 500g
- Olive oil: 1 liter
- Vegetable oil: 1 liter
- Sugar: 2 kg
- Flour: 2 kg
- Salt: 1 box
- Pepper: 1 pack
- Spices: Assorted (cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, etc.)
- Coffee: 500g
- Tea: 1 box (100 bags)
- Juice: 4 liters (1 liter per week)
- Snacks: Biscuits, nuts, chips (as needed)

HOUSEHOLD:
- Toilet paper: 12 rolls (3 per month)
- Paper towels: 4 rolls
- Laundry detergent: 2 bottles (or 4 packs powder)
- Fabric softener: 2 bottles
- Dish soap: 2 bottles
- All-purpose cleaner: 2 bottles
- Glass cleaner: 1 bottle
- Bathroom cleaner: 2 bottles
- Trash bags: 1 box (30L, 30 bags)
- Aluminum foil: 1 roll
- Plastic wrap: 1 roll
- Zip-lock bags: assorted sizes
- Light bulbs: 4 (as needed)
- Batteries: AA and AAA packs

PERSONAL CARE:
- Shampoo: 2 bottles (500ml each)
- Conditioner: 2 bottles
- Body wash or soap: 4 bars or 2 bottles
- Toothpaste: 4 tubes
- Toothbrushes: 4 (replace every 3 months)
- Dental floss: 2 packs
- Mouthwash: 2 bottles
- Deodorant: 2 sticks
- Razors: 1 pack (disposable or cartridge)
- Shaving cream: 2 cans
- Feminine hygiene products: as needed
- Moisturizer: 2 bottles
- Sunscreen: 2 bottles (for summer)
- Hand soap: 4 bottles
- Hand sanitizer: 2 bottles

OTHER:
- Pet food (if applicable): 1 month supply
- Pet treats
- Litter (for cats)
- Cleaning supplies for pets
- Stationery: notebooks, pens, printer ink
- Kitchen supplies: sponges, scrubbers
- Matches or lighter
- Candles
- First aid kit supplies: bandages, antiseptic, pain relievers
- Over-the-counter medicine: cold medicine, antihistamines, etc.

NOTE: Quantities are approximate for one person or a small family. Adjust based on household size, dietary preferences, and consumption habits. Consider buying non-perishables in bulk and perishables weekly for freshness.
